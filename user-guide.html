How to Add an Expense

Tap the "‚ûï Add Expense" button on the Dashboard or in the Week History view.

  • Choose Expense (money going out) or Credit (money coming in)
  • Enter a description (e.g., "Grocery run")
  • Enter the amount
  • Select a category from your Monthly Planning
  • Pick the date ‚Äî the expense auto-assigns to the correct week
  • Optionally split into installments across multiple weeks
  • Tap Add ‚Äî done!
üí° Splitting Expenses

Bought something expensive? Split it across 2, 3, or 4 weeks. The app will automatically create an entry for each week (e.g., "Laptop (1/3)", "Laptop (2/3)", "Laptop (3/3)").

Viewing Your Week Cards

Tap "üìä History" on the Dashboard to see all your weekly expense cards:

  • Use the month/year selectors at the top to navigate between months
  • Swipe left/right between the 4 week cards of the month

  • The remaining balance is shown at the bottom of each card
  • Tap "Current Week" (bottom right) to jump back to this week

Setting Up Your Monthly Budget

Tap "üìÖ Plan" on the Dashboard or "Monthly Planning" in the History view.

  • Enter your monthly salary
  • Add categories with a name, type, and budget amount
  • Choose the type for each category:
    Credit = deductions (market, savings) ‚Äî subtracted from salary
    Spend = discretionary spending that counts against your weekly budget
  • Choose frequency: weekly (budget per week) or monthly (divided by 4)
  • Tap Save ‚Äî categories will appear in the Add Expense picker
üí° How Weekly Budget is Calculated

Weekly categories ‚Üí budget per week as-is.
Monthly categories ‚Üí budget √∑ 4 = weekly allowance.

Linking Your Telegram Account

This enables password recovery through Telegram ‚Äî no email needed.

  • Tap the cat avatar on the Dashboard
  • Select "üì± Link Telegram"
  • A 6-digit code will appear on screen
  • Open Telegram, search for @WeeklyWalletBot
  • Send the code to the bot
  • You'll get a confirmation: "‚úÖ Account linked!"

üîê Your Data is Secure

Weekly Wallet uses end-to-end encryption:

  • All financial data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • Each user has a unique encryption key that is tied to your password
  • Data is stored on Cloudflare R2 ‚Äî fast and globally distributed
  • Nobody (not even the server) can read your financial data
